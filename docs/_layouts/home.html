Reading the numbers

Days behind
Days since stable Electron first shipped a newer Chromium major than the one this app bundles. From that day on, fixes landing only in the newer major are ones the app cannot receive as a patch.
Open CVEs
Chromium CVEs on record with NVD that were fixed in a Chromium build newer than the one the app ships, shown as critical / high. They are fixes that exist and that this release does not have.
green / orange / red
Whether the app's Electron major is still supported, went end-of-life within the last six months, or went end-of-life before that.
